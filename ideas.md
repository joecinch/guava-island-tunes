# Guava Tunes — Design Brainstorm

## Overview
Guava Tunes sells AI-generated Suno songs for special occasions: weddings, funerals, birthdays, anniversaries, etc.

## Idea 1: Warm Tropical Elegance (Art Nouveau meets Tropical)

**Design Movement:** Art Nouveau + Tropical Warmth
**Core Principles:**
- Organic curves and flowing lines inspired by nature
- Warm, sun-drenched color palette evoking celebration and emotion
- Handcrafted feel — like a bespoke gift
- Emotional storytelling through typography and imagery

**Color Philosophy:** Deep guava pink (#C0395A) as primary, warm cream (#FFF8F0) background, gold (#D4A853) accents. Evokes warmth, love, and celebration — perfect for weddings and birthdays. Muted charcoal for funerals section.

**Layout Paradigm:** Asymmetric editorial layout. Hero with large diagonal text offset. Song cards arranged in a masonry-style grid. Occasion categories displayed as horizontal scroll strips.

**Signature Elements:**
- Guava fruit motif as brand icon (stylized, minimal)
- Wavy/organic dividers between sections
- Hand-drawn style borders on song cards

**Interaction Philosophy:** Gentle, unhurried animations. Hover reveals song previews with soft fade. Scroll triggers staggered card entrances.

**Animation:** Fade-up on scroll, gentle pulse on audio play button, wavy SVG dividers animate on load.

**Typography System:** Playfair Display (headings) + DM Sans (body). Italic for emotional taglines.

## Idea 2: Moody Vinyl Record Shop (Neo-Brutalist Dark)

**Design Movement:** Neo-Brutalism + Dark Vinyl Aesthetic
**Core Principles:**
- Raw, high-contrast design with bold borders
- Dark background evoking late-night music listening
- Brutalist grid with intentional "imperfection"
- Typography as the primary visual element

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#0D0D0D) background, electric coral (#FF4D4D) primary, off-white (#F5F0E8) text. Stark contrast. Feels underground, authentic, bold.

**Layout Paradigm:** Strict brutalist grid with thick borders. Song cards as "record sleeves." Navigation as a bold horizontal bar with oversized text.

**Signature Elements:**
- Thick 2px black borders on all cards
- Oversized track numbers
- Vinyl record circle motif on hero

**Interaction Philosophy:** Snappy, instant transitions. Hover inverts card colors (black/white flip). Click feels like pressing a physical button.

**Animation:** No easing — instant state changes. Marquee scrolling text for song titles. Glitch effect on logo hover.

**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (headings, all-caps) + IBM Plex Mono (metadata). Monospace for prices.

## Idea 3: Soft Celebration Studio (Warm Pastel Boutique)

**Design Movement:** Contemporary Boutique + Warm Minimalism
**Core Principles:**
- Soft, celebratory warmth without being saccharine
- Clean whitespace with deliberate pops of color per occasion
- Premium gift-shop feel — like a curated music boutique
- Emotional color coding per occasion type

**Color Philosophy:** Warm ivory (#FDFAF5) base, guava coral (#E8614A) primary CTA, sage green (#7A9E7E) for secondary, dusty rose (#D4A0A0) for weddings, slate blue (#6B7FA3) for funerals, golden yellow (#F0C040) for birthdays. Each occasion has its own accent color.

**Layout Paradigm:** Left-anchored asymmetric layout. Hero with large left-aligned headline and right-side featured song card. Occasion categories as pill-shaped filter tabs. Song grid with generous spacing.

**Signature Elements:**
- Color-coded occasion badges on each song card
- Floating audio waveform visualization on hover
- Subtle grain texture on background

**Interaction Philosophy:** Smooth, warm transitions (300ms ease-out). Audio preview on hover with waveform animation. Cart slides in from right.

**Animation:** Staggered card entrance on scroll, waveform pulse on play, smooth color transitions between occasion filters.

**Typography System:** Cormorant Garamond (display headings, italic) + Outfit (body, UI). Elegant contrast between serif display and clean sans-serif.
